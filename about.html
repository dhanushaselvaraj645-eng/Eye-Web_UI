Hospital Introduction

A Multi-Specialty Eye Hospital You Can Trust

Netra Vision Eye Hospital was founded with a single goal: to make world-class eye care accessible, affordable and comfortable. Today we operate modern diagnostic and surgical facilities staffed by fellowship-trained ophthalmologists across every sub-specialty.

From a child's first eye check-up to complex retina surgery for seniors, our teams are equipped to handle the full spectrum of eye conditions under one roof, with NABH-accredited safety standards.

Our Mission

To eliminate avoidable blindness and vision impairment by delivering timely, high-quality and compassionate eye care to every patient who walks through our doors, regardless of background.

Our Vision

To be recognised as the region's most trusted eye hospital — known equally for surgical excellence, patient comfort, and continual investment in the latest ophthalmic technology.

Our Story

Two Decades of Restoring Sight

2005 — The Beginning

Netra Vision opened as a small 10-bed clinic with two ophthalmologists and a single OT, focused on cataract care.

2011 — Introducing LASIK

We launched our LASIK and refractive surgery unit, becoming one of the first in the region to offer bladeless laser vision correction.

2016 — NABH Accreditation

Netra Vision received full NABH accreditation, reflecting our commitment to clinical safety and quality management.

2020 — Retina & Glaucoma Centre

A dedicated retina and glaucoma centre was established with OCT imaging and advanced laser platforms.

2026 — 60,000+ Surgeries

Today, our 45+ specialists have performed over 60,000 successful surgeries with a 98% patient satisfaction rate.
